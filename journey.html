Konica Minolta's 2022 Strategy

Konica Minolta's DX strategy encompasses two significant business transformations occurring in parallel. Konica Minolta will continue to grow its core business and evolve the office products line of business into the Digital Workplace business. Traditional office products will be redesigned to support the Interconnected Workplace with solutions that digitize paper-based documents and automate business processes. At the same Konica Minolta will actively invest and develop growth in the measurement, inspection and diagnostic fields. Profitable market sectors such as Industrial Print, Healthcare and Industry will also be pursued
