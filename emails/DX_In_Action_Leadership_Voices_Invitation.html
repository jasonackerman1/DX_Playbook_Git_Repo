Rethink Konica Moinolta's Digital Transformation «Leaders across the organization share their stories...»

Konica Minolta's Digital Transformation journey evolved over a number of years. In an effort to engage all employees in this global initiative, the Learning Group is developing a DX Playbook. The DX Playbook is a repository of learning experiences that will provide all employees with an understanding of what DX means and why their individual contributions are integral to the success of our company, our customers and our employees. The DX playbook is also a resource of continuous learning and a highly interactive platform to start meaningful conversations with your teams and colleagues.
